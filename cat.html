What We’re About

Sharing simple, friendly info for new and seasoned cat parents—no fluff, just the good stuff.

Why Cats?

They’re graceful, independent, and hilarious. Also: tiny toe beans. Enough said.

Starter Kit

  • Litter box & clumping litter
  • Quality wet/dry food
  • Scratching post & toys
  • Carrier & ID tag

Feeding

Stick to a routine. Fresh water daily. Ask a vet before switching diets or adding treats.

Health

Annual checkups, vaccines as advised, and keep an eye on appetite, grooming, and litter habits.

Environment

Vertical spaces, scratching options, and quiet nooks make for a confident, happy cat.
